Super Smash Flash 2

The Super Smash Flash 2 game features a variety of scenarios, each with its unique gameplay. You will find yourself facing four bad opponents with a variety of moves and abilities. The game has two attack buttons and a variety of different attacks. Four players can play the game simultaneously on the same computer. Whether you want to play solo or with a friend, this game offers the perfect combination of fun and challenge. It is not for the weaker heart!
